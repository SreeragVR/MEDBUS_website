PRICING

One Platform.
Clearly Defined Roles.
Complete Control.

Medbus is built around role-based access control (RBAC)—ensuring every user sees only what they need, nothing more. This improves security, accountability, and efficiency across your clinic or hospital.

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Right Access. Right Role. Right Time.

Each Medbus module is mapped to user roles with granular permissions. Admins can control who can view, create, edit, approve, or delete data—down to individual actions.

Improved data security

Clear responsibility & accountability

Faster workflows for staff

Frequently Asked Questions

01.

Is Medbus suitable for multi-branch setups?

Yes. Manage branches, users, and consolidated reports from one place.

02.

Can I migrate my existing data?

Absolutely. We help import patients, services, price lists, and more.

03.

Do you offer support?

Yes—email/chat/phone support based on plan, plus a knowledge base and training.

04.

What about offline access?

Core features are cloud-first; selective offline workflows can be discussed for on-premise deployments.

Yes—secure video, e-prescriptions, and payments can be enabled where supported.
